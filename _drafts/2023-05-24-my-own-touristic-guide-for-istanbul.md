I wanted to create my own touristic guide for Istanbul myself for anyone.

* Overview Of The City

Istanbul is a huge city. It's the one of the most crowded cities in the world. 
Not the all of İstanbul considered touristic naturally. There are different Istanbul's of course. We will focus on touristic and cultural war.
They always talk about poor people who never see the see. 

For cultural and touristic purposes we can trim some and choose some districts of Istanbul. But we can consider Boğaz and around is Istanbul for practical purposes.
For european side we can count Bakirkoy, Fatih (Old City), Beyoglu, Besiktas (Touristic line. And Dolmabahce Palace. Levent Business District.), Şişli (Nisantasi, Bomonti), Sariyer.
For anatolian side we can count Beykoz, Üskudar, Kadikoy and Moda and Prince Islands.

## Galata Bridge

Even I lived close to it. And see it again and again I think Galata Bridge amazes me the most. It also has a lot of tourists.
The center is mostly a Galata Bridge even Leonardo Da Vinci created a design.
Current bridge connects old Istanbul (Sultanahmet, Fatih, Eminönü) with a new (Karaköy, Beyoğlu, Harbiye).
